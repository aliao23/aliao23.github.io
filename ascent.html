Ascent

Branding for an Australian esports tournament.

Info

Fall 2018

Client — Amateur Oceanic Esports

In late 2018, I was approached by AOE to create a logo for Ascent, their newest Overwatch tournament celebrating the Oceanic esports community. AOE hosts the largest competitive Overwatch community tournaments in the OCE region (the Amateur Australian Overwatch League), with over 500 participating players of varying ranks every season.

Context & concepts

Ascent is AOE’s senior league: focusing more competitive integrity than balanced, even matches in an arena for teams looking to prove themselves and test their strength against other teams. AOE wanted something that would reflect its existing brand visuals. Its AAOL series logo emulates the Overwatch logo, using an outer circle with an isolated arc (albeit inverted from the original); its own branding also uses an indigo-red gradient.

I wanted to reference elements of both in Ascent, so I began experimenting with different forms and color combinations in Illustrator. Players often talk about plateaus and peaks, and with “ascent” suggesting some form of elevation or climb, I decided that a mountain would probably be the most representative symbolically for an esports tournament. I initially wanted to use the letter A as a base, but a more abstract shape worked better overall in context of the Overwatch style.


Moving on up

AOE liked the latter design, so I started iterating on the smaller details of the logo — in particular, adding depth to the “peak” and including gradient variations in the circle around it to finish up.


I also considered creating alternate text variants in a similar vein to the AOE logo, but ultimately these were too divergent from the original brand intention.
